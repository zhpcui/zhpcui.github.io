Short Bio

I am currently a senior researcher working with Prof. Marc Pollefeys in the Computer Vision and Geometry Group at ETH Zurich. I received my Ph.D. degree in computer science under the supervision of Prof. Ping Tan at Simon Fraser University. Before that, I was a Ph.D. candidate supervised by Prof. Ping Tan at National University of Singapore. I obtained my Bachelor degree and Master degree at Xidian University in 2009 and 2012 respectively. During my internship, I was luckily mentored by Dr. Jue Wang, Dr. Oliver Wang at Adobe and Dr. Jinwei Gu, Dr. Jan Kautz at NVIDIA.

My research interests include computer vision, computer graphics, robotics, and machine learning.
